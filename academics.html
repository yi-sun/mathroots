ACADEMICS



Learn by doing

At MathROOTS, students will discover new mathematical ideas and learn problem solving skills through a series of classes, group activities, and invited lectures led by a team of instructors with diverse experiences teaching both research and competition math.

The program features:

  • Creative problem solving outside the standard curriculum
  • Exposure to beautiful yet accessible ideas in mathematics
  • Introduction to techniques and meaning of mathematical proof
  • An academic and cultural community of like-minded peers, mentors, and instructors
