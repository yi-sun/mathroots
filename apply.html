APPLICATION



MathROOTS is open to all students who satisfy the following criteria:

  • US citizens or permanent residents
  • Between the ages of 14-18
  • Demonstrated academic excellence, particularly in mathematics
  • Students should be comfortable with high school algebra, geometry, trigonometry, exponents, and logarithms. Precalculus is strongly recommended. Calculus is not required.

Women, underrepresented minorities, and students from economically disadvantaged backgrounds are very strongly encouraged to apply.

All educational, food, and boarding costs are generously covered by our funders. However, students are required to pay for their transportation to and from MIT. Travel scholarships are available on a limited basis for students with need.

The application for MathROOTS 2016 will open in early March 2016.
